Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Ghana?

The best time to visit Ghana is during the dry season from November to March when the weather is cooler and there's less rainfall. This is ideal for outdoor activities and wildlife viewing.

Do I need a visa to visit Ghana?

Most visitors need a visa to enter Ghana. Citizens of ECOWAS countries don't need a visa. We recommend checking with the Ghanaian embassy in your country for the most current visa requirements.

What vaccinations do I need?

Yellow fever vaccination is mandatory. We also recommend vaccinations for hepatitis A & B, typhoid, and malaria prophylaxis. Consult your doctor for personalized medical advice.

What currency is used in Ghana?

The Ghanaian Cedi (GHS) is the official currency. US Dollars and Euros are widely accepted. Credit cards are accepted in major hotels and restaurants in Accra and other major cities.

Is it safe to travel in Ghana?

Ghana is considered one of the safest countries in West Africa. Like any destination, we recommend taking standard travel precautions and staying aware of your surroundings.

What should I pack for Ghana?

Pack light, breathable clothing, comfortable walking shoes, sunscreen, insect repellent, and any necessary medications. Don't forget your camera to capture the amazing experiences!
