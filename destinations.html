Discover Ghana's Beautiful Destinations

From bustling cities to pristine nature reserves, Ghana offers unforgettable experiences

Accra - The Vibrant Capital

Experience the heart of modern Ghana

Accra, Ghana's bustling capital, is a city where traditional culture meets modern urban life. From the historic Jamestown to the vibrant Osu district, Accra offers a perfect blend of old and new.

Top Attractions

  • Independence Square
  • National Museum
  • Makola Market
  • Labadi Beach
  • Kwame Nkrumah Memorial
Best Time: November - March
Starting from $299

Cape Coast - Historic Gateway

Walk through centuries of history

Cape Coast is a city steeped in history, home to the famous Cape Coast Castle and beautiful beaches. This UNESCO World Heritage site offers a profound journey through Ghana's past.

Top Attractions

  • Cape Coast Castle
  • Elmina Castle
  • Kakum National Park
  • Fort William
  • Cape Coast Beach
Best Time: December - February
Starting from $199
Book Cape Coast Tour

Kumasi - Cultural Heart of Ghana

Experience the Ashanti Kingdom

Kumasi, the former capital of the Ashanti Empire, is the cultural heart of Ghana. Known for its rich traditions, colorful markets, and royal heritage, Kumasi offers an authentic Ghanaian experience.

Top Attractions

  • Manhyia Palace
  • Kejetia Market
  • Cultural Centre
  • Prempeh II Museum
  • Bonwire Kente Village
Best Time: November - April
Starting from $249
Book Kumasi Tour

Tamale - Gateway to the North

Discover Northern Ghana's beauty

Tamale is the largest city in Northern Ghana and serves as the gateway to the region's unique culture, wildlife, and landscapes. Experience a different side of Ghana in this vibrant northern city.

Top Attractions

  • Mole National Park
  • Tamale Central Market
  • Dakpema Palace
  • Gulkpegu Sacred Grove
  • Shea Butter Cooperatives
Best Time: October - March
Starting from $199
Book Tamale Tour

Volta Region - Natural Paradise

Waterfalls, mountains, and scenic beauty

The Volta Region offers some of Ghana's most spectacular natural attractions. From the highest waterfalls to scenic mountains and the largest artificial lake in the world, this region is a nature lover's paradise.

Top Attractions

  • Wli Waterfalls
  • Mount Afadja
  • Lake Volta
  • Tafi Monkey Sanctuary
  • Akosombo Dam
Best Time: November - April
Starting from $179
Book Volta Tour

Kakum National Park - Canopy Adventure

Walk above the rainforest

Kakum National Park is famous for its canopy walkway, suspended 40 meters above the forest floor. This unique experience offers breathtaking views and encounters with diverse wildlife in their natural habitat.

Top Attractions

  • Canopy Walkway
  • Forest Elephant Viewing
  • Bird Watching
  • Nature Trails
  • Butterfly Sanctuary
Best Time: November - March
Starting from $149
